Jeremy Alain, as Still Winters, crafts soundscapes that echo the tranquility and depth of quiet, thoughtful nights. His latest self titled album is an abstract journey through sound, embodying the emotions of solitary reflection under the starlit skies of cold Vancouver winters. “Still Winters is a musical testament to the artist’s nights spent wondering, and it invites us all to embrace a contemplative journey through sound, emotion, and the human spirit.” - Stefan Baranowski (Insight Music Blog). Also known for his work as Kysiko, Jeremy’s experimental sound has infused abstract soundscapes with artists like Boslen — leading to a JUNO nomination for his ‘GONZO’ project — and Aaryan Shah’s ‘A Love Letter To LA’. Continuously redefining sound, he envisions his unique cinematic atmospheres moving into film and visual storytelling.
